Careers

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

We are always looking for trained and experienced tutors for in-class as well as online sessions. If you are passionate about teaching music and mentoring students, we would love to hear from you.

What We Are Looking For

  • Strong subject expertise in vocals, instruments, or music production
  • Prior teaching or coaching experience (in-person and/or online)
  • Clear communication, patience, and a student-first mindset
  • Professionalism and commitment to scheduled classes

Responsibilities

  • Conduct structured lessons based on academy curriculum
  • Guide students with technique, practice routines, and progress tracking
  • Prepare students for performances, exams, or showcases
  • Collaborate with the academy team to maintain quality standards

How to Apply

Please share a short introduction, your teaching background, and the area you specialize in. You can reach us via the Contact page and we will get back to you.
