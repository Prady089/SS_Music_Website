Skills & Outcomes

Strong Musical Foundations

Build correct technique, posture, coordination, and control from the very beginning.

Rhythm, Timing & Accuracy

Develop a solid sense of timing, groove, and precision essential for all styles of music.

Music Reading & Understanding

Learn to read notation, follow charts, and understand musical structure with confidence.

Performance & Collaboration

Gain the skills to play with others, support a song, and perform in ensembles or bands.

Confidence & Creative Growth

Improve focus, discipline, and self-expression while enjoying the process of making music.

Curriculam

Vocal

Bridge Academy (Carnatic)

Breath & voice technique, raga & swara training, kriti repertoire, improvisation (neraval/kalpana swaram), tala and stage performance.

Piano

Trinity College London (Classical)

Music reading, scales & technical work, graded repertoire, sight‑reading, aural skills and exam preparation.

Keyboard

Trinity College London (Rock & Pop)

Chordal harmony, comping, contemporary repertoire, improvisation, arrangements and ensemble skills.

Violin

Trinity College London (Classical)

Posture & bow control, intonation, shifting, vibrato, classical repertoire and ensemble performance.

Guitar

Trinity College London (Rock & Pop)

Chords & strumming, riffs and lead technique, improvisation, song support and ensemble playing.

Drums

Trinity College London (Rock & Pop)

Rudiments, limb independence, groove & timing, fills, dynamics and supporting a band in multiple styles.
